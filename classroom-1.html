Classroom

Did you know that you have a talent for teaching? You for sure do! Anytime you need to explain something to a single person or even to a whole group of interested people, you have use for this room.

Especially when you need a small group of individuals to gather on a small place, to stay in chat range, you find this room handy. You will become a professor and your students will listen to you.

There is a large desk in front of you where you can put your things, students have their own desks. Two study seats and two places for visitors. A screen to play your videos from Youtube. In this small room 10 people plus a teacher can stay.

Fully rezzed the scene occupies 115 prims, house included and it is 9.6 x 0.8 m2 large.
