About

I hope you will enjoy your gift but please read more, before you will try it. (A lot of prims to be rezzed!)

What you got is a rezzer of a multipurpose house in Second Life. The multi-scene rezzing system, as well as most of the objects I share with you, you can find on Marketplace. Inside your house you can rezz the following:


5 furnished rooms
- 28 prims = empty house; the empty house is already included in:

*Gallery 
*Christmas Theme - 131 prims
* Classroom - 115 prims
* Pub - 148 prims
* Living Room - 83 prims

How to use it
1. Go to some sandbox or to areas with many prims available.
2. Rez the house. It has 28 prims when it is empty.
3. Go inside the house and press the MENU above the doors.
4. Pick the room you want to see (The current themes are: Christmas, Pub, Classroom, Living room
5. Select Remove from menu when you go. You can delete the rezzed empty house. You have a copy in your inventory.

- Use the house empty and furnish/edit it according your needs.
- Use the ready to use rooms to invite your friends, students or visitors and have all the options by hand in any situation at almost any sandbox.
- Copy any individual item(s) to your inventory you like, use the items wherever you may need them.

Permissions are mostly: copy+mod (all items are copy)
Most items inside seem to be no mod, but you can delete a script inside and you will be able to modify most of them.
(The script is located in the content and servers for the items to be rezzed, its name is: Multi-Scene Object Pin v3.5.3)


Creators:
