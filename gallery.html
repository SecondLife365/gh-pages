This room is furnished by furniture usable in living rooms, bed rooms and other individual rooms where people stay to spend time, for example with their friends by a cup of coffee. You can invite your friends and have them seated by the table while chatting with them.

Most items are copy/mod. When you need, just take or copy them to your inventory and use anywhere you need. inside of every object, there is a script Multi-Scene Object Pin. You do not need that script when you do not use the object with the rezzer. Feel free to remove Multi-Scene Object pin from the content.
