New! (changelog)

Changes made since January 2020

01/2020

  • Works on this documentation started, the website was created.
  • The script changed for doors. Now it stays open. The script is full perm and I got it for 150 L. It allows to lock the house for a list of users or for a group

12/2019 - version 5.1


  • Tableware

- Inside the house there is a menu, pick the Xmas room.
- On the table there is a new box. Click on it to uncover your new Tableware.
- Tableware has its own menu, pick your favorite meal in it.
- Use any items to decorate your own places, or take the whole rezzer.
Talbleware menu will work without the original house too. Use anywhere.

  • New room - Frame Shop (Gallery)


- Inside the house there is a menu, pick the frameshop.
- A new room appears with many new gifts including 50 square shaped picture frames.
- These frames were updated this year and they are not yet on the Marketplace: you are first who gets them!
